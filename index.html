Mission

AI-Agent is a novel concept that seeks to address the complexity of human biology by integrating molecular, cellular, tissue, organ, and systemic dynamics into a unified, hierarchical AI framework. Our mission is to advance the development of AI-driven agents for analyzing complex biological systems, enhancing data-driven discoveries, and accelerating translational applications in medicine, biotechnology, and life sciences. By fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, we seek to push the boundaries of AI-powered biological research and its real-world impact.

Call for Participation

We invite researchers, industry experts, and practitioners to contribute to the discussion on AI-driven biological analysis and innovation. Submissions are welcome in areas such as AI-powered single-cell analysis, multi-omics integration, multi-modal imaging, drug discovery, synthetic biology, and autonomous scientific discovery. Join us to present your latest findings, engage in dynamic discussions, and collaborate with leading minds shaping the future of AI in biology.
